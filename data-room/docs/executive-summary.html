The solution

Three technology silos. One ear tag. 48-72 hours of warning.

CacheCow converges three independently validated technologies into a single on-device system that detects illness 48-72 hours before any visible symptom — with 89% accuracy, running entirely on-device.

Silo 01

BCC — Body Channel Communication

Bioelectric signals conducted through the animal's own tissue. Sub-milliwatt power. Measures conductivity changes that precede inflammation by days.

Silo 02

Tunnel magnetoresistance sensors detecting the earth's magnetic field through animal movement. Orientation, gait, posture, and micro-tremor patterns at nanoscale precision.

Silo 03
