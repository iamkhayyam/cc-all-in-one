06 — Revenue Projections

Three layers. Five years. One compounding machine.

Hardware revenue from Year 1. Data licensing from Year 2. Platform IP from Year 3. Each layer compounds the economics of the one before it.

Revenue streams

Three revenue layers with different economics and timelines.

01

Hardware + Subscription

$95 device cost, $50/yr subscription. 65%+ gross margin. Recurring revenue with 5-20 year contracts. Foundation layer that generates data for layers two and three.

Active from Year 1

02

Data Licensing

Anonymised herd intelligence sold to insurers, pharmaceutical companies, and genetics programs. Zero cost of goods. Near-100% margin. Weather data integration at 10% participation.

Activated Year 2

03

Platform IP Licensing

ARM-style licensing of ternary AI, bioelectric sensing, and neuromorphic compute to adjacent verticals. Wearables, sports, healthcare, industrial. Near-100% margin.

Activated Year 3
