Not a dashboard. A decision engine.

Every animal tagged gets an AI model trained specifically to its individual baseline. The subscription is what keeps that model learning, updating, and sending alerts. It doesn't monitor the herd. It knows the animal.

Flags physiological deviation before symptoms are visible. Intervention cost drops from $2,500 to $150. On average, 89% detection accuracy.

Breeding intelligence

Estrus prediction at 95%+ accuracy

12–24 hours advance warning vs. reactive detection. Optimises conception rates and reduces repeat breeding costs by $400–800 per animal annually.

When animals lie down, the device shifts to full compute mode. Models update nightly. Every morning the system is smarter than it was the day before.
