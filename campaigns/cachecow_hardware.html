Revenue Stream 01 — Hardware + Subscription

The tag pays for itself.
The subscription is pure margin.

Device sales and per-animal annual subscriptions form the foundation of the business. Every tag deployed recovers its cost within the first year through prevented losses alone — and each one becomes a permanent data node feeding the higher-margin layers that follow.

What the subscription delivers

Not a dashboard. A decision engine.

Every animal tagged gets an AI model trained specifically to its individual baseline. The subscription is what keeps that model learning, updating, and sending alerts. It doesn't monitor the herd. It knows the animal.

Health intelligence

48–72 hour illness prediction

Flags physiological deviation before symptoms are visible. Intervention cost drops from $2,500 to $150. 89% detection accuracy.

Breeding intelligence

95%+ estrus prediction accuracy

12–24 hour advance warning. Optimises conception rates. Reduces repeat breeding costs by $400–800 per animal annually.

Infrastructure

No cell coverage required

Self-forming mesh between devices. No gateways, no internet. Runs in -40°C to +85°C. Built for real pastures, not tech demos.

Overnight learning

The herd teaches itself while it sleeps

When animals lie down, devices shift to full compute. Models update nightly. Every morning the system is smarter than yesterday.

Why this revenue stream exists

Hardware and subscription are designed to be the smallest of the three revenue streams.

Every device deployed generates data. That data is the raw material for licensing revenue that requires no additional cost of goods. Revenue stream one funds the collection of the asset that powers revenue streams two and three.

Every tag deployed is a permanent node in the Motion Intelligence Index.

The more animals enrolled, the more valuable every individual operation's data becomes. Hardware gets us onto the ranch. The index is what makes us impossible to leave.
